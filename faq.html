Frequently asked questions

Answers to the questions we're asked most often. Can't see what you're looking for? Get in touch and we'll be happy to help.

General

What does Civil Digital do?

Civil Digital designs and builds custom digital solutions for businesses and organisations. We specialise in Android apps, web applications, business process automation, Microsoft Power Platform solutions, and bespoke software tailored to your needs.

Who do you work with?

We work with small businesses, sole traders, charities, and any organisations looking to improve efficiency through technology.

Where are you based?

Civil Digital is based in Bedford, United Kingdom, and works with clients across the United Kingdom and internationally.

Can you work remotely?

Yes. Most projects are delivered remotely using online meetings, collaborative planning, and regular progress updates.

Software development

Can you build a custom app for my business?

Yes. Every business is different, so we create bespoke applications designed around your workflows rather than forcing you to adapt to off-the-shelf software.

Do you develop Android apps?

Yes. We develop modern Android applications using current technologies, with integration into cloud services such as Firebase where appropriate.

Can you build web applications?

Absolutely. We build secure, responsive web applications that can be accessed from desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Can you integrate with existing systems?

Where possible, yes. We can integrate with APIs, Microsoft services, Firebase, payment providers, and other third-party platforms.

Microsoft Power Platform

Do you build Microsoft Power Apps?

Yes. We create Power Apps that digitise paper processes, simplify data collection, and improve productivity.

Can you automate repetitive tasks?

Yes. Using Microsoft Power Automate, we can automate approvals, notifications, data transfers, and many other manual processes.

Working together

How much does a project cost?

Every project is different. After discussing your requirements, we'll provide a clear quotation with no hidden costs.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. We can provide ongoing maintenance, updates, bug fixes, and feature enhancements after your project has been delivered.

Can you improve an existing application?

Yes. We can review your current software, identify improvements, fix issues, and add new functionality.

How long does development take?

Timescales depend on the complexity of the project. Smaller solutions may take a few weeks, while larger systems may take several months.

Security & quality

Will my data be secure?

Security is considered throughout development. We follow good development practices and implement appropriate authentication, access controls, and secure data storage.

Who owns the finished software?

Unless otherwise agreed, you own the software developed specifically for your project once contractual obligations have been met.

Getting started

I have an idea but don't know where to start. Can you help?

Absolutely. We can help turn your idea into a practical solution by understanding your goals, recommending the best technology, and planning the project with you.

Do I need a detailed specification before contacting you?

No. Many clients come to us with just an idea. We can help define the requirements and recommend the best approach.

How do I get started?

Simply get in touch through our contact form or email us with a brief description of your project. We'll arrange an initial discussion to understand your requirements.
