About Civil Digital

A specialist digital consultancy for businesses and organisations — practical, affordable and business-led.

Who we are

Civil Digital is a UK digital consultancy that helps businesses and organisations deliver meaningful digital transformation. We combine Microsoft Power Platform expertise, custom application development and pragmatic consulting to solve real operational problems — without unnecessary cost or complexity.

Our philosophy

We are business-led, not technology-led. Decisions start with the outcome you need and the people doing the work, and only then move to the technology. Our guiding principle is simple: we choose what's right for the task, not what's right for us. That means sometimes recommending a small change rather than a big system, or an existing tool rather than something new to buy.

Understanding your environment

We understand the realities of running an organisation — tight budgets, accountability, procurement, accessibility obligations and the need to keep essential day-to-day operations running. That context shapes everything we recommend, so solutions are realistic to adopt and sustainable to support.

How we work

Our engagement is consultative. We take time to understand the problem, assess the available options honestly, recommend the best-fit solution for your budget and goals, then deliver and support it. You get clear advice in plain language at every step — no jargon, no pressure.

Why clients choose us

  • Flexibility — we adapt to how you work and the constraints you face, rather than forcing a fixed methodology.
  • Affordability — high-quality delivery without enterprise consultancy costs.
  • Honest advice — we recommend the right solution, even when it earns us less.
